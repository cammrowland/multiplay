About Multiplay

How does Multiplay work?

Videos play directly in your browser and are never uploaded to any server. All content stays on your device.

Why use Multiplay?

Perfect for comparing different versions of the same video, checking edits side-by-side, or reviewing before/after changes. Ideal for video editors, content creators, and design teams who need to make precise comparisons with synchronized playback.

For best results, use Google Chrome.

Desktop Site Recommended

Multiplay is designed to compare videos side-by-side, which works best on a laptop or desktop computer.

The experience on mobile devices may be limited due to screen size constraints.

For best results, use Google Chrome.
